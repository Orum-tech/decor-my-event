Common Questions

How do I book a vendor?
Simply browse our 'Vendors' section, choose a service that fits your style and budget, and click 'Book Now'. You'll be connected directly to verify availability and finalize details.
Is there a booking fee?
No! Using the Decor My Event platform is completely free for customers. No fees will be taken directly from you. You pay the vendor directly as per agreed terms.
Can I customize the decoration packages?
Absolutely! Most of our vendors offer customizable packages. You can discuss your specific color themes, flower choices, and layout preferences directly with them after connecting.
What if I need to cancel?
Cancellation policies vary by vendor. We recommend discussing the cancellation and refund policy with the specific vendor before making an advance payment.
